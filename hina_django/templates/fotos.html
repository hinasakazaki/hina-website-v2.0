I got a camera in the beginning of March and have been enjoying taking photos. I have many hobbies, like music, swimming, drawing, eating, but... I guess it was a very impulsive move for me to buy a beginner DLSR after thinking about it for about a month.

My pre-teen hobby was to take videos with my camcorder and make movies. At one point I aspired to be a music video director. Since I broke my camcorder in the Summer after my high school graduation, I had taken photos with my smartphone.

It's very interesting to me how photography is evolving, along with the Smartphone revolution. I recently downloaded Snapchat, and it's been an enlightening experience. What is value of photos? An ephemeral "share"? Or a part of a portfolio?
